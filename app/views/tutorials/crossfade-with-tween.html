Task Runner provides a large selection of built-in tasks. One of these is the task. Tween allows you to tap into requestAnimationFrame and create optimized tween effects. This tutorial shows you how simple it is to create these effects.

The Interval task accepts a callback and a duration (in milliseconds). The callback is where you describe your animation. Interval runs this callback once per animation-frame and passes it a 0-1 value, indicating how far your animation should have progressed.
